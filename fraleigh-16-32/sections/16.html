Definitions 4
  • A left action of G on X is a map \(G\times X\to X\) with \(e x=x\) and \((gh)x=g(hx)\).
  • The orbit of x is \(Gx=\{gx\mid g\in G\}\). It is x가 작용으로 도달 가능한 모든 위치다.
  • The stabilizer of x is \(G_x=\{g\in G\mid gx=x\}\). It is x를 고정하는 G의 부분군이다.
  • A G-map \(f:X\to Y\) satisfies \(f(gx)=g f(x)\) Bijective G-map은 G-set isomorphism이다.
Theorems 3

Orbit partition theorem

The orbits of a G-set X form a partition of X: every x lies in exactly one orbit, and two orbits are either equal or disjoint.

Proof spine. Define \(x\sim y\) iff \(y=gx\) for some g. The action laws give reflexive, symmetric, transitive. Equivalence classes are exactly orbits.

Use. 서로 다른 상태 수를 셀 때 먼저 X를 orbit들로 찢는다.

Orbit-stabilizer theorem

For x in X, the orbit Gx is in bijection with the left cosets \(G/G_x\). Hence, if G is finite, \(|Gx|=[G:G_x]=|G|/|G_x|\).

Proof spine. Send \(gG_x\) to gx. This is well-defined because ghx=gx for h in G_x. Conversely \(gx=kx\) implies \(k^{-1}g\in G_x\), so \(gG_x\)=kG_x.

Use. 몇 개로 움직이는지와 몇 개가 고정하는지는 곱해서 |G|가 된다.

Transitive G-sets are coset spaces

If X is transitive and x0 in X, then X is isomorphic as a G-set to \(G/G_{x_0}\).

Proof spine. Map gG_{x0} to gx0. Orbit-stabilizer의 대응이 G-action과도 호환된다.

Use. transitive action 문제는 사실 coset action 문제로 바꿔 풀 수 있다.

Propositions 2

Stabilizers are subgroups

For every x in X, G_x is a subgroup of G.

Proof spine. e fixes x. If g,h fix x, then gh fixes x. If g fixes x, then \(g^{-1}\) fixes x by applying \(g^{-1}\) to gx=x.

Conjugate points have conjugate stabilizers

If \(y=gx\), then \(G_y=gG_xg^{-1}\).

Proof spine. k fixes gx iff \(g^{-1}\)kg fixes x.

Examples 3

Left regular action

G acts on itself by g*x=gx. The orbit of any x is all of G, and the stabilizer is {e}.

Use. This is the cleanest model of a free transitive action.

D4 acting on square vertices

Let D4 act on the four vertices of a square. The action is transitive; the stabilizer of one vertex has two elements, so the orbit size is 8/2=4.

Use. 대칭군 문제에서 stabilizer를 세면 orbit 크기가 즉시 나온다.

Coset action

G acts on G/H by g*(aH)=(ga)H. The stabilizer of H is H itself.

Use. 이 예시는 모든 transitive action의 표준형이다.

Exercises 4

Orbit check

Let G act on X. Prove that x and y are in the same orbit iff Gx=Gy.

Proof spine. Use \(y=gx\), then show each element of Gy is also in Gx and conversely.

Stabilizer computation

For the natural action of S3 on {1,2,3}, compute the orbit and stabilizer of 1.

Proof spine. Orbit is all three points; stabilizer consists of permutations fixing 1.

Coset model

If X is transitive and |G|=24, |G_x|=6, how many elements does X have?

Proof spine. Orbit-stabilizer gives |X|=4.

G-map test

For a conjugation action of G on itself, decide whether f(x)=x^{-1} is a G-map.

Proof spine. Check \(f(gxg^{-1})\) against \(g f(x)g^{-1}\).

Proof Patterns 2
  • To prove an action is well-defined, check representative choices before checking the two action axioms.
  • To prove a stabilizer statement, start from k(gx)=gx and multiply by \(g^{-1}\) on the correct side.
